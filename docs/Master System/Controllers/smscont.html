Sega Master System Controllers

Joystick/Thumbpad

9-pin female D-sub connector on the controller.

  1. /North
  2. /South
  3. /West
  4. /East
  5. +5VDC Supply
  6. /Button 1
  7. -
  8. GND
  9. /Button 2

Direction/button pins are held high by the console, and are forced low by actions on the pad.

Genesis pad

The Genesis pad is really a superset of the SMS pad. It's almost identical, but adds a couple things.

9-pin female D-sub connector on the controller.

  1. /North
  2. /South
  3. /West or CLK mirror
  4. /East or CLK mirror
  5. +5VDC Supply
  6. /B or /A
  7. CLK
  8. GND
  9. /C or /Start

While the console has the CLK line set high, the directions and buttons B and C are read.

When the consule pulls the CLK line low, pins 3 and 4 are pulled low by the controller to indicate to the console that a Genesis controller is attached, then buttons A and Start are read.
